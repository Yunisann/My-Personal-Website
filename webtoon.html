💗 WEBTOON 💗

Webtoons (Korean: 웹툰) are a type of digital comic that originated in South Korea.They are usually meant to be read on smartphones. Unlike traditional comics or manga, webtoons are designed to be read vertically by scrolling down on a computer or smartphone screen.Each episode typically takes less than five minutes to read, making them accessible and convenient for readers. You can find a wide variety of genres on platforms like WEBTOON, which hosts comics, manga, and manhwa from around the world with every genre we like.
